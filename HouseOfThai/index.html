Hello and Welcome to Our House.

House of Thai offers casual family dining and is conveniently located off the US-35 Fairfield Road and Research Drive exits in Beavercreek. Just minutes away from Wright-Patterson AFB, House of Thai is the perfect lunch destination, take-out stop on the way home, or dine-in experience. Our convenient and easy parking, relaxed atmosphere, and family-style dining offer the perfect, intimate venue to catch up with old friends, enjoy a date night out, or conduct an important business meeting.

Meet Chef Mam

The Woman Behind the Food

At House of Thai we take pride in providing the most authentic Thai experience we can. We go to great lengths to ensure all our food is prepared to the peak of freshness. We don’t use any prepackaged foods. All our food is fresh and prepared for you daily. We only use the freshest vegetables and meats and know that it will create that authentic Thai flavor that our customers have come to love.

Chef Mam has been preparing loving Thai food for over fifteen years in top Thai restaurants in Ohio. She has experimented with different flavors since she was a kid even owning multiple prestigious restaurants in Thailand. Her culinary pallet has been refined over many years and she loves sharing her love of food with people and you can really taste it in her food.

Her favorite dish to make at home is Khao Mun Gai which is a traditional chicken and rice dish prepared with a spicy sweet soy sauce dip.
